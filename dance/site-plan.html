Overview

Purpose

This site will provide many useful resources for individuals to learn American ballroom dances and dance steps. Such resources will include organized music and playlists, instruction videos, and a web-based tempo finder.

Audience

Our audience consists of any individuals interested in learning American style ballroom. This site will be most specifically taylored to students enrolled in DANCE-180 at Brigham Young University - Idaho.

Dynamic elements

Dynamic elements will include music sorted by dance style, videos sorted by dance style, and dances that match a recommended tempo.

Content

Home page

C-Swing is a collection of resources to help individuals learn American style ballroom (often referred to as social dance). From Mambo to Foxtrot and from Waltz to Swing, we have a library of resources to help you get moving!

Our library of music has been tempo-corrected to ensure easy dancing!

Our instruction videos were designed with the beginner in mind. If you have no dance experience, don't you worry!

* I plan to have a brief video on loop on the home page *
